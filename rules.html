Scoring Rules

The scoring is based on recommendations from the American Heart Association and the National Institute of Health, which recommend 150 minutes of moderate intensity activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week.

This is done to encourage building a healthy habit and not just binge-working out one day a week.

  • There is a maximum of 50 points allowed per day.
  • There is a maximum of 150 points allowed per week.

How Points Are Scored

  • 1 point per minute with your heart rate in Zones 1-3.
  • 2 points per minute with your heart rate in Zones 4-5.

Heart Rate Zone Calculation

To calculate your zones, you need your max heart rate and resting heart rate (RHR). First, subtract your RHR from your max heart rate to determine your Heart Rate Reserve (HRR). The zones are then calculated as follows:

  • Zone 1: RHR + (0.4 * HRR) (Itâ€™s usually 0.5, you are welcome for the grace)
  • Zone 2: RHR + (0.6 * HRR)
  • Zone 3: RHR + (0.7 * HRR)
  • Zone 4: RHR + (0.8 * HRR)
  • Zone 5: RHR + (0.9 * HRR)
